Google Form Field Name Inspector

Instructions:

  1. Open this page in your browser
  2. Open your Google Form in a new tab:
    Click here to open your Google Form
  3. Right-click on the first input field (Name) and select "Inspect Element"
  4. Look for the name attribute - it will be something like entry.123456789
  5. Repeat for each field and copy the entry numbers below

Alternative Method - Pre-fill URL:

Or try this easier method:

  1. Go to your Google Form editor
  2. Click the "Send" button (top right)
  3. Click the "Link" tab
  4. Click "Get pre-filled link"
  5. Fill out the form with dummy data
  6. Click "Get link"
  7. The URL will show the field names like: entry.123456789=Name&entry.987654321=Email
